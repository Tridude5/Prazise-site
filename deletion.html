Data Deletion

Last updated:

You can request deletion of your data at any time. Because Prazise is currently in pre-launch, the data we hold is typically limited to your waitlist email and any messages you sent us. Below are your options.

Delete via email

Send a deletion request from your account email to slavinskasjack@gmail.com (optional cc: johnslavinskas@my.uopeople.edu).

Tip: include the address you used for the waitlist and any devices you mentioned (e.g., Garmin, Apple Health) so we can find you quickly.

Delete via contact form

You can also use our form—choose “Privacy” as the topic:

Request deletion via contact form

Local browser backup (your device)

If you previously joined the waitlist while no backend was configured, your browser may have stored a local backup of your email (on your device only). You can clear that here:

This clears only local storage keys used for the early demo (e.g., prazise_waitlist). It does not affect any emails already sent to us.

Revoking device connections

If you have connected a third-party provider in the future app (e.g., Apple Health, Google Fit, Garmin, Polar, Suunto, COROS, WHOOP, Oura, Wahoo, Fitbit, Strava), you can revoke access from that provider’s settings at any time. Revoking stops new data from flowing to Prazise. We can then delete any previously stored copies upon request.

When the app launches

The app will include an in-app option to delete your account and associated data (e.g., Settings → Account → Delete). That will remove your profile, training data, and revoke third-party tokens we hold on your behalf.

Identity & timing

  • Identity verification: We may ask you to verify ownership of the email/account requesting deletion.
  • Response time: We aim to confirm deletion within 30 days, sooner whenever possible.

See also our Privacy Policy for details about what we collect and why.
