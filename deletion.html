Data Deletion

You can delete your account and connected data at any time. Choose either method:

  1. In-app: Settings → Data & Privacy → Delete Account (removes profile and training data; revokes third-party tokens).
  2. Email: Send a request from your account email to privacy@yourdomain.com. We will confirm and complete deletion within 30 days.

Revoking a device connection (Garmin/Polar/Suunto/Fitbit/Apple Health) stops new data flow immediately.
